About Me

Welcome to my website! I am an aspiring programmer enrolled in the UT Austin Coding Bootcamp. Over the next few months, I will be learning how to be a full-stack software engineer and complete a number of projects to cultivate my skills.

Prior to enrolling in the coding bootcamp, I graduated from the University of Chicago with degrees in mathematics and economics and worked as an investment banker in San Fracisco for three years. I was born and raised in Austin and am excited to be back in the live music capital of the world. During my free time, I enjoy reading, watching sports, and spending time in the outdoors.
